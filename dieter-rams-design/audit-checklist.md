## Quick Reference: Rams Quotes

Use during audits as standards:

| Principle | Quote |
|-----------|-------|
| Core philosophy | "Less, but better." |
| On confusion | "Impenetrable confusion of forms, colours and noises." |
| On usefulness | "A product is bought to be used." |
| On aesthetics | "Only well-executed objects can be beautiful." |
| On understanding | "At best, it is self-explanatory." |
| On unobtrusiveness | "Neither decorative objects nor works of art." |
| On honesty | "It does not attempt to manipulate." |
| On longevity | "It avoids being fashionable." |
| On thoroughness | "Nothing must be arbitrary or left to chance." |
| On environment | "Minimises physical and visual pollution." |
| On reduction | "Back to purity, back to simplicity." |
