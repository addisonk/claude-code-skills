The Layout Catalog

Ten named arrangements that fill the 16:9 slide canvas — so a section reads like a slide, not a wall of text pinned to the top.

One idea, one metaphor

Editorial split

A magazine-style 60 / 40 grid: prose holds the left column, a visual or callout anchors the right. Both columns are vertically centered in the canvas.

Use it when an idea needs a paragraph of explanation and a supporting figure side by side.

How to use a layout

  1. Pick the archetype that matches your content shape.
  2. Add class="slide layout-name" to the section.
  3. Drop in the matching primitive — the layout centers it.
